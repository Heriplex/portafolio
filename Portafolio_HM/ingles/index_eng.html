About me

Biografía

Hello! My name is Heriberto Mansilla, I'm a technology professional with over 18 years of experience in the IT support field. I'm originally from Puerto Montt, a city I love, and currently, I work as the IT manager at Hotel Vicente Costanera. My professional career began in the accounting area, but after a while, I realized that my calling was in information technology. After working in computer maintenance and repair at various industry locations, I moved on to provide user support at Trusal, which led me to deepen my knowledge in network administration, obtaining my title as a Network Administrator from IP La Araucana.

What drives me in my work are technology, security, and personal growth. I take pride in my sense of responsibility, my ability to work in teams, and my commitment and loyalty to the company I work for.

I firmly believe in the importance of continuous improvement and follow the Kaizen philosophy in my professional and personal life. I am committed to furthering my professional development in the field of technology and facing every challenge with determination and perseverance. I live each day with passion and dedication, always seeking to learn and grow both in my career and in life.
